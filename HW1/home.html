BALIKESİR

Balıkesir is located west of the Turkey in the Marmara region. he province of Balikesir has pretty little towns of wide natural beauties.
The beaches extending for kilometers on both the Aegean and Marmara coasts, the dazzling waters of deep blue, and rich greenery gently covering the land
attract anyone who would like to have a wonderful time in the midst of wonderful scenery.It has a population of 331,788 inhabitants. Between 1341–1922, it was the capital of Karasi.
